Our Goal

Sustainability for your windows, Long-term energy efficiency.

Normatt can customize an inspection program to assess the present operating condition of a building's window system -- focusing on replacement windows installed after 1985. Windows manufactured by Traco, Graham, Wausau Metals, Seasonal, Republic, Peerless, EFFCO, Crystal, and others can be inspected, cataloged, diagnosed, and rated for performance. A list of specific repairs and parts required to return a building's windows to optimum condition will be provided. Finally, a complete analysis of the window system, along with all details deriving from the inspection will be rendered in a comprehensive report. Included in Normatt's package is a Thermal Imaging exterior analysis which will be performed during the months of November through March.

  • 30 Years Experience in commercial window system design and installation
  • Proven inspection program developed for the Chicago Public School system
  • Customized proprietary software for analyzing field data
  • State-of-the-art equipment
  • Professional, mature, and experienced field personnel
  • AIA architect on staff
  • Reasonable pricing structure
  • Lead-detection -- certifed inspection and assessment
  • User-friendly final report, analysis, and recommendations

Our aim is to provide a building owner/manager with a variety of tools and services to accurately measure the efficiency and level of performance of a building's current window system. Our services will provide a database and site analysis with which a building owner/manager can plan repairs, modifications, or the replacement of a building's windows thus extending the life-cycle of these critical assets.
