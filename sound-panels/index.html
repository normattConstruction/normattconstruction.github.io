Sound Proof Glazing

NoiseGuard Panels

Normatt can provide a complete, architectural-grade line of acoustical, sound suppression interior panels to augment your present windows. This secondary system is engineered to work in concert with your present windows to reduce the transmission of exterior sound by 70% to 80%.

Your existing windows remain as-is. There is no construction mess because the new panels are discretely installed to the interior portion of the window opening. The panels and frames are nearly invisible.

We feature panel units manufactured by Allied Window of Cincinatti, Ohio, the acknowledged leader in the development and production of secondary-glazing (sound-proof and energy panels) for over 40 years.

If noise pollution is a concern in your home or office, please call us to analyzie your problem (traffic, airplanes, trains, or street noise), and provide a provide a quiet solution.

Our field engineers can precisely calculate current decibal levels and recommend the proper fixed or operating sound proof panel(s) to address your requirements.

In soundproof applications, 3/16" or 1/4" laminated glass or polycarbonate panels will be employed as our standard.

As a bonus, our soundproof panels will also significantly increase your current windows' energy efficiency and thus help pay for themselves over time.

In fact, very few other captial improvements can deliver an R.O.I. (return on investment) as impressive as these panels.

Please contact us to arrange a site visit and see if Soundproof Panels can solve your "noise" problems.
