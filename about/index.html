About Us

Normatt Construction Services, Inc. was established to provide a full spectrum of window-related services to its clients. Ranging from on-site inspection, analysis and repair of existing windows to complete replacement, to the installation of state-of-the-art sound-proofing and energy loss prevention panel systems (interior secondary glazing). Normatt can fashion a solution to any window problem being experienced.

Since 2004, Normatt has worked with entities such as the Chicago Board of Education and the Archdiocese of Chicago, helping to manage their extensive window assets.

Windows Matter
Windows comprise at least 1/3 of a building's envelope and can be the primary cause of energy loss thru air-leakage and radiation of heat/cooling to the exterior.

By analyzing the condition of a building's existing windows, cost-effective repairs and designed on-going maintenance protocols can be executed, allowing under-performing windows to be restored to an optimum operational state.

Normatt's services are designed to assist architects, building owners and property managers in maximizing the operation and energy-efficiency of windows in a given building.

Normally, windows comprise an average 35% to 45% of a building's exterior envelope. Poorly maintained windows will result in significant energy loss (winter and summer) and therefore waste money 24 hours a day, every day.

Just as an HVAC system requires regular adjustment and professional maintenance to ensure proper function, so too the windows. Even though windows represent a significant capital expenditure, they are rarely ever considered in a building's maintenance program.

Allow Normatt to help you manage your buidlings windows and demonstrate mathematically how energy-loss through your windows can be cut dramtically.
