Energy Panels

EnergyGuard Panels

Normatt can now provide state-of-the-art interior, commercial-grade Energy Panels (secondary glazing system) to our residential and institutional customers. Our engineering staff can design a custom-fit Energy Panel application to decrease BTU loss thru the existing windows by 40% to 60%.

Our Energy Panels can deliver increased energy-efficiency by augmenting your existing windows, not replacing them. Our energy panels deliver cost-effective performance in an understated design which blends seamlessly with any decor or window configuration.

Your existing windows remain undisturbed. there is no disruptive construction mess or inconveinance. Our Energy Panels are designed to fit discretely to the inteior and to match existing sight-lines.

Low-E coated, poly-carbonate, laminated, tinted or other glazing options are available to address any customer need or specification.

Please contact us for an on-site consultation.

  • Eliminate drafts and air infiltration.
  • Reduce heat/cold loss thru windows (radiation).
  • Augment your present windows -- don't replace them.
  • Save money and lower your "carbon-footprint."
